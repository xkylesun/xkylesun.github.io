About Me

I am a software engineer with experience in both front-end and back-end web development. My main areas of focus are in React, Redux, Node.js, and Ruby on Rails. Recently I have been working on various projects including a deep clone of crowdfunding site Kickstarter, a deck-building game with random map generation, and a tower defense game built on Javascript. In addition, I had been working as an associate specializing in employment and mobility tax in a big-four accounting firm.


For my personal life, I have a passion for mountain sports including hiking and snowboarding. I also share an enthusiasm in Sci-fi movies and learning new technologies. I am currently interested in learning to develop games with Unity engine.
